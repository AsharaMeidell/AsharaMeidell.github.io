About Me.

A Girl With A Smile

I am a recent Western University graduate (B.A., Media and the Public Interest) who is passionate about social justice, the environment, mental health, and personal development. I am a major advocate for critical thinking and questioning the world around us. I believe that thoughtful use of technology and media can support positive social change.

I have a variety of experience in communications roles working for non-profits and businesses. My interests include writing, multimedia creation (graphic design, video, animation etc.), and project management.

My personal interests include travelling (I recently spent a month backpacking Europe), creative writing, cooking, pilates, and learning everything I can.
